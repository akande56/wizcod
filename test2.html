About Us

Wiscod (Women for Women International activists) is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering women and girls through education, advocacy, and community engagement. Our mission is to create a world where women have equal opportunities and are active participants in shaping their communities.

Our Programs

  • Women Peace Network
  • Adult Literacy Sessions
  • Livelihood Activities (Catering, Makeup, Hairdressing, Tailoring)
  • Cosmotology Classes for People with Disabilities
  • Small Scale Business Empowerment Grants
  • Menstrual Care and Hygiene (MCC) Program for Teenage Girls
  • Safe Emergency Delivery Caregivers Program

Recent Activities

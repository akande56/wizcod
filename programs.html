Our Programs

Women Peace Network

Our Women Peace Network brings together women from diverse backgrounds to promote peace, conflict resolution, and community development. Through regular meetings and training sessions, we empower women to become active peacebuilders in their communities.

Adult Literacy Sessions

We offer adult literacy classes in Babale, Jos North LGA of Plateau State, and other areas. These sessions aim to improve reading, writing, and basic numeracy skills among women, enabling them to participate more fully in their communities and pursue economic opportunities.

Livelihood Activities

Our livelihood program provides training in various skills to help women achieve economic independence. Courses include:

  • Catering
  • Makeup and Hairdressing
  • Tailoring

These classes equip women with practical skills they can use to start their own businesses or find employment.

Cosmotology Classes for People with Disabilities

We offer specialized cosmotology classes for people living with disabilities, ensuring that everyone has the opportunity to learn valuable skills and pursue their passions.

Small Scale Business Empowerment Grants

Our grant program provides financial support to women entrepreneurs, helping them start or expand small-scale businesses. This initiative aims to foster economic growth and independence among women in our communities.

Menstrual Care and Hygiene (MCC) Program

Our MCC program focuses on educating teenage girls about menstrual health and hygiene. We conduct monthly meetings and awareness sessions in three project implementing areas:

  • Babale Jos North
  • Mai Adiko Jos South
  • Gindiri Mangu LGA in Plateau State

Safe Emergency Delivery Caregivers Program

This program trains community members to provide safe emergency delivery care. We hold monthly meetings for caregivers in:

  • Babale Jos North
  • Mai Adiko Jos South
  • Gindiri Mangu LGA in Plateau State

ensuring that women have access to critical support during childbirth.

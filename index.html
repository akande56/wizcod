About Us

WISCOD is a Community Based Non-Profit Organization established in 2004 to address the issues of women and the less privileged in our society. We are non-political, interfaith, and focused on driving value propositions for women, girl-children, and the less privileged through social and economic empowerment.

Our Focus Areas

  • Conflict transformation and peace building
  • Improved Reproductive Health and Reduced spread of HIV/AIDS
  • Education and literacy programmes for disadvantaged groups
  • Women and youth empowerment, and skill acquisition livelihood programmes

Our Projects

Women for Women International (2021-2025)

This project focuses on women and girls in border communities affected by conflicts in Jos North and Jos South LGAs of Plateau State. We've trained 150 women as mediators, tracked gender violence, improved community policing, and provided empowerment skills to 140 women and girls.

Mennonite Central Committee Funding (2023-2026)

This project aims to build the capacity of Safe Emergency Delivery Care givers in Jos North, Jos South, and Mangu LGAs. We focus on reducing child maternal morbidity and mortality through Home Based Life Saving Skills and prevention of mother-to-child HIV transmission.

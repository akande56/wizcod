Building a Viable Community and Reducing Maternal and Child Mortality

2019 - 2023

Key activities include:

  • Advocacy visits to project areas
  • Capacity building trainings for Peer Educators
  • Safe Emergency Delivery Care givers (SEDC) training
  • Teenage Girls monthly interactive sessions
  • HIV counseling and testing
  • Production and distribution of Reusable Sanitary Pads

Increasing Women's Participation in Peace and Security

2021 - 2024

This project includes:

  • Capacity Building trainings for Women Peace Network members
  • Establishment of Women Peace Network in project areas
  • Basic and Post literacy classes
  • Livelihood Activities in Baking, Catering, Make-up, Hair Dressing, Tailoring, and Cosmetology
  • Village and Savings Association Group (VSLA)

About WISCOD

Profile

The Women Initiative for Sustainable Community Development (WISCOD) Is a Non – governmental is a Community Based Non-Profit Organization established in 2004 to address the issues of women and the less privileged in our society. It is non - Political, Interfaith and non- Profit oriented organization focus on driving value propositions for women, girl-child and less privileged through social and economic empowerment. The drive to create a community where the rights of women and less privileged are realized is our main drive and strength.

We ­have achieved some strides in various communities through initiatives that helps to alleviate mental, physical and financial poverty. Our consistent approach and methodologies are driven by the models which are specific to immediate communities where our beneficiaries are concentrated. We apply local knowledge, language, best form of traditions, mediations and intervention principles which are in line with global best practices for social development and humanitarian supports.

Objective

To create an enabling environment for communities to strive for available resources within their reach to enhance the development of their community

Our Vision

To create a community where women and less privileged persons are empowered to realize their rights and achieve their full potentials.

Our Mission

To exploit underdeveloped human and material resources through training and awareness/sensitization programmes to improve and enhance community potentials for women and the less privileged.
